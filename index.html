Work Experience

Project Leader • May 2014 - Ago 2014

I developed a protocol to improve the performance of PVC formulations in extrusion process. This plan consisted of the optimization and standardization of PVC tube formulations for all chemical plants in Mexico. This protocol reduced scrap by 20% on sewer pipe formula to yield possible savings for 1Million pesos per year.

I did estimations of profit from PVC tube production to analyze the impact in cost reduction that my project would have. I created strong connections with suppliers to improve formulas and reduce costs.

Chemical Engineer Intern • Jun 2011 - Aug 2011

In this internship I investigated and evaluated the industrial plant production process for cosmetics and personal care products. I had to acquire the skills to sample separate and identify compounds present in the products. Finally, I was able to propose and test new products that were innovative to the company.

Research Intern • May 2012 - Aug 2012

I participated in the Bioengineering Research Lab where I helped develop a project to obtain ethanol and bio-plastics out of aliment waste created by a food plant. Then, I assisted with data collection and analysis of results to improve the performance of the process.

After doing research, understanding the results and making changes to the process, I was able to increase the efficiency of the enzymatic hydrolysis by 15%.
